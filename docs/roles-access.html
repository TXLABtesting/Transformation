1. The four roles

Every user belongs to exactly one role. The delivered build opens as ممثل الجهة by default (NEXT_PUBLIC_DEFAULT_ROLE); in production the role comes from the sign-in mapping against the users table. All scoping below is enforced centrally in the view-model — one place decides what each role sees.

1

منسق المسار في الجهة — Coordinator

Purpose: the data owner. Creates and maintains every item of ONE stream inside their entity and plans the launches.

Pages seen

  • Dashboard — stream-scoped: totals row (إجمالي، المشاريع/المبادرات، العمليات/الخدمات as applicable to the stream), percentages row (الإنجاز، التحول الذكي المساعد، الأتمتة، المكتمل), per-مرحلة summary (items · عمليات · execution cost · each launch with its budgets), item cards/list.
  • Add wizard — method (manual / bulk) → type (per stream: مشروع/مبادرة everywhere; عملية in العمليات والدعم المؤسسي and العمل الحكومي الاستراتيجي; خدمة in الخدمات) → 5 steps ending with خطة التنفيذ والإطلاق (مرحلة + launch plans + وضع العنصر).
  • Bulk upload — the official workplan Excel; parsed rows reviewed before submission.
  • إدارة خطط الإطلاق — collapsible launches per مرحلة: name, type, date, attached items, derived execution budget (read-only), manual launch budget, scope.
  • Item detail — full data; can edit drafts, update execution checklist and launch completion.

Data visible

  • Only items of their entity AND their stream — including drafts and returned items (بحاجة إلى تعديل).
  • Execution budgets and launch budgets of their stream's launch plans.
  • Funding outcome (معتمد للتمويل) — but not the nomination activity of رئيس المسار.

Actions

  • Create / edit / submit items (name mandatory; scope + execution budget mandatory at submission).
  • Delete drafts and withdraw & delete submissions not yet approved (سحب وحذف).
  • Manage launch plans; attach/detach items (one مرحلة per item); bulk-assign selected cards to a مرحلة.
  • Update execution checklist status and mark launches done (completes the item).

2

ممثل الجهة — Entity representative

Purpose: the entity's official gate. Approves what the coordinators submit and tracks the entity's whole portfolio and costs.

Pages seen

  • Team setup wizard (first sign-in) — entity fixed from UAE PASS; rep name, email (mandatory), phone; stream owners in the official stream order.
  • Dashboard — entity-wide: totals row with per-stream distribution inside the المشاريع/المبادرات and العمليات cards (zeros shown), percentages row + ميزانية التنفيذ التقديرية and ميزانية الإطلاق التقديرية (للاطلاع), per-مرحلة summary with each launch's budgets, cards/list.
  • Item detail — read-only data view with the full audit log and approval actions.
  • Team panel + program deadlines editor.

Data visible

  • All streams of their entity, from submission onward — drafts are never visible.
  • Both cost figures: execution totals (what the committee counts) and launch totals (awareness only).
  • Funding outcomes; not the nomination pipeline.

Actions

  • Approve a submission (اعتماد) → item becomes مخطط · المرحلة N.
  • Return with notes (إعادة بملاحظات) → item goes back to the coordinator as بحاجة إلى تعديل.
  • Maintain the team and program deadlines; export the entity portfolio.
3

رئيس المسار — Stream head

Purpose: national owner of one stream. The five heads are predefined officials (names/titles shipped in the app; accounts linked by IT).

Pages seen

  • Dashboard — their stream across all entities, with an entity filter; KPI cards relevant to the stream.
  • Item detail — read-only, full data of approved items.
  • Nomination basket (سلة الترشيحات) — their nominations awaiting the committee.

Data visible

  • Only approved items (drafts and pending entity approvals are hidden) of their stream, across all entities.
  • Execution budgets; nomination and funding status of their stream's items.

Actions

  • Select items → ترشيح للتمويل (nominate to the committee); withdraw own nominations before funding.

4

اللجنة الوطنية — National committee

Purpose: national oversight and funding against the approved 100M AED budget.

Pages seen

  • Dashboard — national analytics: participating entities, totals, pending/funded counts, average transformation score (with the scoring formula tooltip), the budget bar (approved / spent / remaining / utilisation), recommendation classification (موصى به / قائمة الانتظار / غير موصى به), stream + entity + status filters.
  • Committee basket — items nominated by رؤساء المسارات and the funded list with total cost.
  • Item detail — read-only, full data + audit log.

Data visible

  • Everything approved, across all entities and streams (drafts and pending approvals hidden).
  • All execution budgets; spent = sum of funded items' execution budgets only (launch budgets never counted).

Actions

  • Fund nominated items (or fund directly); decline nominations; cancel funding with a mandatory reason (the entity is notified).
